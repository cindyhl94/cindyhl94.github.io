COVID-19

Our response to Coronavirus

We are taking various measures to maintain the safety of our clients, workers and community during the Coronvirus

Firstly, we encourage workers to complete the 30 minute COVID-19 infection control training provided by the Department of Health. Workers should maintain standard health and safety precautions when supporting individuals in the home and community. This includes following state guidelines on social distancing rules and using personal protective equipment, if necessary.

Last updated 23/05/2020.
