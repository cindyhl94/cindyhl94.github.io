Kindred /'kɪndrɪd/ (adjective): a group of persons related to another; like a family, tribe or clan.

At Kindred Health Group, we're on a mission to help individuals be their best selves. We support individuals to achieve their goals for physical, social and mental wellbeing. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our focus is on four key areas:

Connection

Freedom to choose your own workers based on interests and skills

Community

Support to help individuals develop their sense of community

Capability

Greater independence achieved through assistive technology and capacity building

Clarity

Providing clarity for individuals and their families

Social
Support

Support to attend social activities. Technology assistance to help stay connected online

School
and Work

Assistance to achieve goals related to school/education or the work place

Home
Support

Help with meal preparation and other home tasks (morning, evening and overnight support)

Holiday
Support

Support to plan, participate and enjoy local travel
